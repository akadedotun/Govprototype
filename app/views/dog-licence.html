Register your dog and apply for a licence

Use this service to register your dog with Ferndale Council and apply for a dog licence.

Before you start, you'll need:

  • your dog's microchip number — a 15-digit number on your registration card
  • the owner's date of birth and email address
  • your home address and postcode
  • a debit or credit card to pay the licence fee

Licensing your dog is a legal requirement in Ferndale for all dogs aged 8 weeks and over.
